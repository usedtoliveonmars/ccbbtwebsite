Clay County Bikes, Blueways and Trails

We are a grassroots citizens group dedicated to promoting projects intended to improve bike paths, trails, roads, and waterfront facilities countywide to the benefit of residents, visitors, and economic development.

Join us as we connect Clay County with bike trails and waterways!
